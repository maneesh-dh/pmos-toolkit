Risk

  • Substrate-manifest fanout drift — if a SKILL.md mentioning the substrate manifest is missed, future consumers of build_sections_json.js won't be told it's available. Mitigation: T2 verification step.
  • regex-DOM walker brittleness — non-greedy regex on <section>…</section> can mis-pair on nested sections. Mitigation: stack-based balanced-tag tracker (same pattern as chrome-strip.js); self-test in T1 against the live 01_requirements.html.
  • Reviewer-prompt back-compat — existing reviewer subagents trained on the old prompt may not produce the quote field. Mitigation: FR-5's hard-fail is fail-loud; the soft-phase dialog explicitly notes "the reviewer may need re-prompting" as a Retry path.

  6. Phase 6a skill-eval rubric: 100% [D] + [J] pass on iter 1 or 2.
  7. Phase 7 /verify: full suite green.
